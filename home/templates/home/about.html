About Us

Camp Rover is a premier cage free dog day care and training paradise! Our indoor/outdoor camp is beautiful, fun, safe and supervised 24/7. We feature over 17600 square feet of indoor and outdoor play area for your dog to romp, play and socialize to their heart's content! Watch your camper online as they run on the spacious grassy area, cool off in the custom waterfall/splashpond, relax on the sun deck or under the cooling misters on a hot day. We also offer dog training for those pups that need to brush up on their etiquette. Your pup will love it, and so will you. Your pup will receive exceptional care by our well-trained, attentive staff who are certified in pet CPR and first aid. They are also exceptional snuggles and belly rubbers!

Day Care

Our daily day care schedule is designed to meet your dog's needs and your busy schedule. Available from 7am to 7pm, you can drop off your pup before work and they will be ready for you when you get off work. Whether you choose to drop them off periodically as needed, a few days a week, or every workday, you know your pup will have exercise, enrichment and socialization in a safe, caring cage-free environment!

Training

We offer a variety of dog training for dogs aged 12 weeks and older. Our trainers use a holistic approach of play & train that focuses on socialization, manners and care in addition to traditional obedience training. Classes offered in group or private settings to best accommodate you and your pup. Has your dog already attended a dog training program but needs more reinforcement? Our refresher classes reinforce and maintain training as well as teach dogs new things.
